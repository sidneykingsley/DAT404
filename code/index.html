Web Design

The most recent websites I have designed and created.

Callum Gardner

Callum is a musician in the London Area who I used to know through family friends. He approached me a year ago asking if I could create a website for him to expand his brand.

Callum's industry is very social and therefore we agreed important for him for it to include plenty social links and updates as well as a photo gallery. I also realised after listening to some of his music in the design/concept stage that I wanted the website to appear playful and creative, mirroring the nature of his music due to the slight edge of it. I did this through the bright colour scheme and use of subtle animations such as the blur-clear hover on the homepage.

www.callumgardnerofficial.co.uk

This Website!

I designed and created this website from scratch, as I did Callum's, using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. As far as libraries go I used the inbuilt CSS3 'Grids' framework and one line of JQuery for the scrolling animation.

I have also created other webpages to display my programs created in JavaScript, but they are in the applications section. I have created numerous additional websites throughout the years, but I haven't put these up on a server.

Graphic Design

This is a selection of works created for my sixth form course.

Album Cover Assignment

In this assignment we were asked to create a fictional series of album covers for the band Muse's song "Uprising". In these designs I aimed to capture the emotive nature of the song; specifically, the anger portrayed and the energy evoked by the sense of 'revolution'.

I used a combination of different techniques such as long-exposure photography and mixed-media. I also included painting, I did this in a style inspired by Pollock's drip painting; due to the emotional and chaotic nature of his paintings which I felt was mirrored in the song.

Magazine Covers

This assignment was a chance to challenge our ability to really conceptualise the subject, Rosa Parks, and think of unusual ways to represent what we think the subject represents in the medium of layout, while keeping it interesting.

Typography

I experimented a lot into typography throughout the years. Perhaps my most significant design, for me, would be the concrete letters I created for the Rosa Parks assignment (above). I also experimented with liquorish, nails and newspaper cuttings.

I enjoy creating typography as it is a unique and powerful way to interact with the user and portray your message.

Application Design

Marine Weather

This is a web-based application I created using JavaScript and the .p5 library which took data from APIs and visualised it in an easy to read and aesthetically pleasing way. I hoped this program would communicate a lot of different data simply and easily; making it possible for use while on the water.

I used an API key from worldweatheronline.com which has unfortunately run out as I was using an educational trial; this means I can't link the actual application, but I have included these screenshots. I also used Google's map API which, with some regex work, I was able to use to translate entered place names into coordinates (as required by the weather API).

This was a very enjoyable project focused on UX/UI which gave me the opportunity to really try and cater to my perspective audience and practice my creativity in design and also add small playful touches. However, I ended up simplifying my designs a lot as you can see to increase functionality and fit into my time schedule. I also really enjoyed the mathematic side to the programming and the challenge of problem solving within it.

For the data visualisation:

  • The wind direction was portrayed by the direction of the particles
  • The wind speed by the speed travelled
  • The water temperature by the icon background colour: blue to red
  • The cloud cover by the canvas background colour: blue to white
  • The weather description in text and icon as well as in icons inside the particles
  • Swell height was communicated by the height of the canvas

Communication App

Aside of my JavaScript programming I studied .Net at college in Computer Science where I created a tenant to landlord interfacing system. This application used automated emails to transfer important messages from landlord to clients and vice versa as well as providing easily accessible information in a clear and user-friendly method. I came up with the concept, designed and coded this application myself as with all of the other programs mentioned.

Sound Visualisation and Video Manipulation

In this assignment we were asked to create a video or audio manipulation program in the node-based application Cycling 74's Max MSP. I created a function which took sound from the inbuilt microphone and decrypted it into frequency and volume values which I then scaled and used to manipulate a flock algorithm in reaction to the sound. I also used physical inputs from an Arduino with hacked added physical components to manipulate visual values of a live webcam.
